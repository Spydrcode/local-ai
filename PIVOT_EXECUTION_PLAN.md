## Risks & Mitigation Strategies

### Risk 1: Agencies won't pay

**Mitigation:**
- Offer 14-day free trial with 10 free reports
- Charge founding member pricing ($99 instead of $299)
- Provide money-back guarantee (30 days)
- Only target agencies that already sell strategic consulting

### Risk 2: AI output quality isn't good enough

**Mitigation:**
- Position as "first draft from junior analyst" (not final deliverable)
- Allow full editing before export
- Provide best practices guide for reviewing/customizing reports
- Continuously improve prompts based on feedback

### Risk 3: Market is too small

**Mitigation:**
- 200,000+ agencies in US alone (0.1% = 200 customers)
- Expand to consultants, web agencies, SEO agencies (500K+ total market)
- International expansion (UK, Canada, Australia)
- Pivot to adjacent markets (accountants, business brokers, franchise consultants)

### Risk 4: Competitors copy the idea

**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage (build relationships with 50-100 agencies fast)
- Network effects (agencies refer other agencies)
- Deep integrations (API, Zapier, HubSpot)
- Continuously improve AI models based on usage data
