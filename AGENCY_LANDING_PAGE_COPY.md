**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?**
```
A: ChatGPT generates raw text. [Product Name] creates professional, client-ready deliverables
with your branding, structured frameworks (Porter's Five Forces, etc.), and formatted for immediate
delivery. Think: the difference between a Google Doc and a $2,000 McKinsey report.
```

**Q: What if the AI gets something wrong?**
```
A: The AI provides a first draft based on publicly available data and strategic frameworks.
You should review and customize before sending to clients (takes ~10 minutes vs. 5 hours to create
from scratch). Think of it as a smart junior analyst, not a replacement for your expertise.
```

**Q: Can I customize the reports?**
```
A: Yes! You can edit all content before exporting. You can also add your own insights, adjust
recommendations, and include proprietary frameworks. The AI provides the foundation; you add your expertise.
```

```
A: Any B2C or B2B business with a website. We've generated reports for restaurants, law firms,
SaaS companies, healthcare providers, e-commerce stores, and more. The AI adapts analysis to the
specific industry and competitive landscape.
```

**Bottom Line:**

Your landing page needs to speak directly to agencies' pain points (time, scalability, revenue)
and demonstrate clear ROI. Focus on:

1. **Instant credibility:** "Used by 50+ agencies"
2. **Clear value prop:** "5 minutes vs. 5 hours"
3. **Specific ROI:** "$28K added revenue per year"
4. **Low friction:** "14-day free trial, no credit card"

Test, iterate, and optimize based on real user feedback.
